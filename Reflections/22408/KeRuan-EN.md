## Why Do I Urge You to Apply to KeRuan—Regardless of How Many Attempts You've Made?

- **If this is your first attempt:**
Secure a spot at a top-tier "985" university, and you can consider your life's ambitions fulfilled.

- **If this is your second attempt:**
Tempered by the experience of your first try, you are now incredibly formidable. Statistics also show that the success rate for second-time applicants to KeRuan is very high.

- **If this is your third attempt:**
KeRuan offers abundant internship opportunities; once admitted, you can fully redeem all the time you seemingly "wasted" on your previous two attempts.

- **If this is your fourth attempt:**
By this point, you are unencumbered by any distractions or burdens—your only path is forward.

- **If you are already on your fifth attempt:**
After five grueling attempts, you finally gain admission to KeRuan—a triumph that buries the shame of your mediocre undergraduate background beneath a blanket of heavy snow.

## On the Day You Officially Become a Student at KeRuan...

Everything else becomes null and void:

- Your undergraduate degree becomes null and void.
- Your undergraduate major becomes null and void.
- The stars become null and void; the moon becomes null and void; the Milky Way becomes null and void; the entire universe becomes null and void.
- Your loves become null and void; your hatreds become null and void.
- The first half of your life becomes null and void; even your current girlfriend becomes null and void.

That KeRuan acceptance letter will descend like a heavy snowfall, burying every single one of your ignominious past failures.
It is an emotion so overwhelming, it makes you want to claw at your own eyes.
